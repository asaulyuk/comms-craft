# comms-craft

Three books most marketers claim to have read, encoded into a Claude skill.

Ogilvy on Advertising. Influence by Cialdini. Universal Principles of Design 
by Lidwell.

Together, they cover what to say, why people respond to it, and how it lands 
visually. This skill loads all three before Claude generates or critiques 
any marketing communication.

## What it covers

LinkedIn posts, cold emails, landing pages, B2B campaign strategy, image 
generation prompts, brand voice, and persuasive copy of any kind.

Cialdini tells you which levers to pull. Ogilvy tells you how to execute.
Lidwell tells you how to design the surface so the message doesn't get lost on the way in.

## How to use it

Add SKILL.md to your Claude project or reference it in your system prompt.

## Source texts

- David Ogilvy, *Ogilvy on Advertising* (1983)  
- Robert Cialdini, *Influence: The Psychology of Persuasion* (1984)  
- Lidwell, Holden, Butler, *Universal Principles of Design* (2003)
